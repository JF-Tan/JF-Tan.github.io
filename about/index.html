    MPBR: Multimodal Progressive Bidirectional Reasoning for Open-Set Fine-Grained Recognition
    Junfu Tan, Peiguang Jing, Yu Zhu, Liuyu

    Probing the Limit of Heat Transfer in Inorganic Crystals with Deep Learning
    Li, J., Chen, Z., Wang, Q., Yang, H., Lu, Z., Li, G., Chen, S., Zhu, Y., Liu, X., Tan, J., Tang, M., Zhou, Y., Zeni, C., Fowler, A., Z¬®ugner, D., Pinsler, R., Horton, M., Xie, T., Liu, T.-Y., . . . Hao, H.

    • Discovered new thermal conductivity materials and confirmed diamond as the upper limit: Through deep learning-assisted crystal structure prediction and first-order principle calculation, more than 500,000 inorganic crystals were systematically screened, confirming that diamond is very likely to be the most stable crystal with the highest thermal conductivity at room temperature, and discovered more than 20 new materials with thermal conductivity exceeding silicon.
    • Proposed efficient screening methods and established databases: For the first time, metal materials (such as MnV) with both high lattice and electronic thermal conductivity were discovered, and an open database of thermal conductivity materials was built to provide tools and resources for future material design.

    LASER TECHNOLOGY 2024

    Research on laser ultrasonic wavefield based on physical-informed neural network
    Xin YAN ,Kaining YING ,Lunan DAI , Junfu Tan,Zhonghua SHEN ,Chenyin NI

    • Laser ultrasonic field modeling and parameter inversion based on PINN: By introducing wave equation priors, forward and reverse PINN models are constructed, and high-precision wave field reconstruction and parameter deduction can be achieved under low data volume.

    • Improve modeling efficiency and robustness: Compared with traditional neural networks and modeling methods, PINN is less dependent on data sparsity, has a simpler physical modeling process and stronger parameter solving capabilities.

    China Laser 2023

    Research on Laser Ultrasonic Total Focusing Imaging Method Based on ZYNQ Acceleration
    Wu, C., Ying, K., Dai, L., Tan, J., Shen, Z., & Ni, C.

    • The full focusing algorithm based on laser ultrasound introduces directional optimization, which has a higher signal-to-noise ratio and more accurate defect positioning capability than the synthetic aperture method.

    • Through parallel optimization and dual-core design, the algorithm is deployed on the ZYNQ platform, achieving an 86% reduction in time consumption while maintaining the same imaging quality.
